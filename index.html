I'm XiTing, a passionate and creative UI/UX designer.
I specialize in data visualization and digital product design  (apps/websites).

我专注于将用户需求与业务目标转化为直观、简洁且富有视觉吸引力的界面。我乐于探索 3D 建模、动效设计与 AI 工具，不断提升创意表达与设计效率，为作品赋予更丰富的层次与生命力。

I focus on turning user needs and business goals into intuitive, clean and visually appealing interfaces. I love exploring 3D modeling, motion design and AI tools to continuously enhance creative expression and design efficiency, bringing richer depth and vitality to my projects.
