可视化合集

Visualization Collection

汇聚近年可视化设计代表作，聚焦电力能源领域核心场景与需求，在项目中我主要负责三维建模、可视化界面设计，动效输出等。通过三维建模还原真实场景、可视化界面梳理复杂信息、动效设计提升交互体验，助力运维人员高效掌控设备状态、优化管理流程，让电力能源领域的可视化应用既具备实用价值。

Gathering representative works of visual design in recent years, focusing on core scenarios and requirements in the field of power energy. In the project, I was mainly responsible for 3D modeling, visual interface design, dynamic effect output, etc. By using 3D modeling to restore real scenes, visualizing complex information through interfaces, and designing dynamic effects to enhance the interactive experience, it helps operation and maintenance personnel efficiently control equipment status and optimize management processes, making visualization applications in the field of power energy both practical and valuable.
