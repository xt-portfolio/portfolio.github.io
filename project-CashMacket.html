现货市场数据监控

Spot market data monitoring

这是一款面向电力能源行业的智慧运维与数据管理平台，聚焦发电站、电力现货市场的全链路数据监控、异常管理与业务操作，通过轻量化工业 UI 设计 + 场景化信息展示，帮助运维人员、市场运营人员高效完成 “数据查询 - 异常排查 - 业务操作” 的全流程工作，解决传统电力系统信息分散、操作繁琐的痛点。

This is a smart operation and data management platform for the power energy industry, focusing on full chain data monitoring, anomaly management, and business operations for power plants and spot markets. Through lightweight industrial UI design and scenario based information display, it helps operation and maintenance personnel and market operators efficiently complete the entire process of "data query anomaly investigation business operation", solving the pain points of traditional power system information dispersion and cumbersome operation.
